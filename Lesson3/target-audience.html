Target Audience

The website is set up to help the people of Ghana who are in need of weather updates ahead of time to help them plan their daily activities. It also keep records fo past weather updates to help individuals in their research site on mobile devices.

Two Personas

Persona 1

There are a lot farmers in Ghana. A lot of the people who are not farmers have small gardens at their backyard. Weather updates are very important to these people. Things become easy for them if they are able to access the current and future weather updates on their phones or digital devices.

Persona 2

Most of the time we dress in accordance to the existing weather conditions. One knowing how the weather will be like can aid in adequately preparing for work or traveling. Whether to take along an umbrella or jacket.

Scenarios

Scenario One
Is it the right time of the year to start planting cropss?
Scenario Two
Do I need to carry an umbrella to work tomorrow?
Scenario Three

Scenario Four

Scenario Five
