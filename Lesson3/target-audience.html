Target Audience

The website is set up to help the people of Ghana who are in need of weather updates ahead of time to help them plan their daily activities. It also keep records fo past weather updates to help individuals in their research site on mobile devices.

Two Personas

Charlene Asare

The action lives in our minds but slowly fades away without the photos to remind us of them. Charlene has always loved sports. She at a point wanted to be an athlete until her uncle taught photography. She's been enthusiastic about capturing the action. Being able forsee the weather condition is very important to her in her. It enables her adequately prepare and go to work with the right gear.

Paa Kwesi

Paa Kwesi is a farmer from the southern part of Ghana. He is into mixed farming and exports most of his produce to other countries. Paa Kwesi is always on the lookout for the weather conditions to help him decide when to plant crops.
